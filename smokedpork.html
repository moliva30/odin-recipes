Smoked Pork Shoulder

Description

This meal can make a variety of other dishes including pork tacos, pulled pork sandwhiches, and much more

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder (8-10 lbs)
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Rub of your choice
  • Foil or butcher paper

Steps

  1. Pull the pork shoulder out and let it come to room temp. Add a generous layer of rub.
  2. Preheat your smoker to 250 degrees.
  3. Place the pork shoulder fat cap up and place your meat probe directly in the center.
  4. Smoke uncovered until the internal temperatue reaches 170, spritzing with apple cider vinegar every hour.
  5. Once the internal temp reaches 170, remove and wrap the pork shoulder tightly with foil or butcher paper.
  6. Place the pork shoulder pack in the smoker and smoke until the internal temp reaches 195 degrees.
  7. Take the pork shoulder out and let rest for 30 minutes.
  8. Shred and enjoy.
