Freddie's Tacos

Description

Freddie's Tacos are a simple but classic dish made famous from Winslow, AZ.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (93/7% preferred)
  • Corn tortillas
  • Mexican Blend Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the tacos by taking a small portion of ground beef and spreading it evenely on one side of a corn tortilla.
  2. Season the ground beef with garlic salt and fresh cracked pepper
  3. Heat the Blackstone to a medium-high heat. Spread an even layer of olive oil on the surface.
  4. Once heated, place the tortilla/ground beef with the meat side down for 3-4 minutes, or until browned.
  5. Flip the taco over and add cheese. Fold the taco while on the Blacktone
  6. Cook the tacos evenely on both sides (Approx 5-7 minutes per side, or until golden/crispy)
